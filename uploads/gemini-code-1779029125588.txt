IMPRESSION / DIAGNOSIS:

2. Marked mass effect with a midline shift of 7.5 mm and significant surrounding cerebral edema.
3. Findings represent a critical, life-threatening medical emergency.

RECOMMENDATIONS:
Immediate neurological and neurosurgical intervention required for intracranial pressure management and surgical decompression evaluation. Keep patient under close hemodynamic monitoring in the Neuro-ICU.
